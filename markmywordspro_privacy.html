Privacy Policy for MarkMy.words Pro

Effective Date: 9-July-2025

Thank you for choosing MarkMy.words Pro, a product of LetsGoAI ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our desktop application. Our core philosophy is simple: your data is your own. We designed MarkMy.words Pro to be a secure, private space for your writing.

In Short: The Key Principles

  • Your Content Stays With You: All your notes, drafts, and creative work are saved exclusively on your local device.
  • We Cannot Read Your Work: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your user-created content on our servers. Ever.
  • No User Tracking: The app contains no analytics, telemetry, or tracking code. We do not monitor how you use the application.
  • You Control File Access: The app only accesses your computer's file system when you explicitly ask it to (e.g., to import or export a file).
  • License Verification is Handled by Microsoft: To confirm your purchase, the app communicates with Microsoft's licensing servers. This process is subject to Microsoft's own privacy policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

LetsGoAI is committed to user privacy. We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Any user-created content. This includes any text, notes, drafts, or other content you create within MarkMy.words Pro.
  • Personal analytics or usage data. We do not track your activity within the app, such as features used or time spent.
  • Any personal information beyond what is required for licensing (see Section 3).

2. How Your Data is Handled

User-Created Content

All content you create in MarkMy.words Pro is saved directly to your computer's local browser storage (localStorage). This data is not encrypted by our application, as it resides on your own device and is protected by your device's own security measures. This data is never sent to our servers or any third party by our application.

Local File System Access

The application will only access your local file system (your documents, folders, etc.) when you initiate an action that requires it. This includes:

  • Using the "Import" function to open a file.
  • Using the "Export" or "Save As" function to save a file to a specific location.

The app does not scan your files or access any data without your direct and explicit instruction.

3. Third-Party Services: License Verification

To verify that you have a valid license for MarkMy.words Pro, the application communicates with the Microsoft Store licensing servers. This is a necessary step to activate and validate the software.

This communication is handled directly between the application and Microsoft. We do not receive any personal data from this process. This interaction is governed by the Microsoft Privacy Statement. We encourage you to review their policy to understand how they handle your data.

4. Data Security

Since all your personal content is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of its security. We recommend you use standard security best practices for your computer, such as using a strong password, keeping your operating system updated, and using security software.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page: letsgoai.co.uk/markmywords/privacy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the application after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
